Innovation and Incubation Center

An Innovation and Incubation Center is a facility or initiative typically found in universities, research institutions, or startup ecosystems. Its primary purpose is to foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and the development of new businesses. IICs provide resources such as mentorship, workspace, access to funding, and networking opportunities to aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators. These centers aim to nurture startups and help them grow into successful companies by providing guidance, infrastructure, and a supportive ecosystem.
